Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
1 bell pepper, sliced
1 onion, sliced
1 cup broccoli florets
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add sliced chicken breasts and cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add sliced bell pepper, onion, broccoli florets, and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce and hoisin sauce. Pour over the chicken and vegetables, stirring to coat evenly.
  5. Serve hot over cooked rice.

Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Serving Size: 2 servings

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:
8 oz (225g) spaghetti
4 slices of bacon, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 large eggs
1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:
Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.
In a large skillet, cook chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
In a small bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with bacon and garlic. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, using the residual heat to cook the eggs without scrambling.
If the pasta seems dry, add some of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

Cooking Time: 15 minutes
Serving Size: 2 servings

Caprese Salad

Ingredients:
2 large tomatoes, sliced
1 ball fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
Fresh basil leaves
Balsamic glaze
Extra virgin olive oil
Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:
Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving platter, alternating them.
Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil.
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Serving Size: 2 servings

Halo-Halo

Ingredients:
Shaved ice
Sweetened condensed milk
Evaporated milk
Ube (purple yam) halaya
Leche flan (caramel custard)
Cooked sweetened red beans
Cooked sweetened white beans
Nata de coco (coconut jelly)
Kaong (sugar palm fruit)
Macapuno (coconut sport)
Sweetened banana slices
Jackfruit strips
Pinipig (toasted rice flakes)

Instructions:
Fill a tall glass with shaved ice.
Layer the glass with a variety of ingredients, such as ube halaya, leche flan, sweetened beans, nata de coco, kaong, macapuno, sweetened banana slices, and jackfruit strips.
Drizzle sweetened condensed milk and evaporated milk over the layers.
Top with pinipig for added texture.
Serve immediately and mix well before eating to enjoy the combination of flavors and textures.

Buko Pandan

Ingredients:
Young coconut meat (buko), shredded
Pandan-flavored gelatin, cubed
Sweetened condensed milk
Evaporated milk
All-purpose cream
Cooked sago (tapioca pearls), optional
Ice cubes

Instructions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded young coconut meat and cubed pandan gelatin.
Add sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and all-purpose cream to the bowl. Adjust the sweetness according to your preference.
If using, add cooked sago (tapioca pearls) for additional texture.
Mix all the ingredients until well combined.
Add ice cubes to the bowl to chill the dessert.
Serve the buko pandan chilled in individual bowls or glasses.
